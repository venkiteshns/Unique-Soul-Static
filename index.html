GST Registration

Assistance in obtaining GST registration for businesses.

Guidance on the documents required for registration.

Handling amendments or cancellations of GST registration.

GST Return Filing

Filing of monthly, quarterly, and annual GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR- 3B, GSTR-9, etc.).

Compliance with deadlines to avoid penalties.

Preparation and submission of error-free returns.

GST Compliance and Advisory

Expert advice on GST laws and their applicability to your business.

Ensuring that your business remains compliant with evolving GST regulations.

Identification and rectification of errors in previously filed returns.

Input Tax Credit (ITC) Management

Assistance in maximizing eligible Input Tax Credit.

Verification of vendor invoices to ensure compliance.

Rectification of mismatches in ITC claims.

E-Way Bill Generation

Assistance in generating and managing e-way bills.

Ensuring compliance with e-way bill regulations for transport of goods.

E-Invoicing Assistance

Guidance on the e-invoicing system for eligible taxpayers.

Setting up e-invoicing systems and integration with existing software.

Representation and Liaison with Tax Authorities

Representing clients in case of notices or audits.

Liaising with tax authorities for any GST-related issues or disputes.

GST Refunds for Exporters

Assisting exporters in claiming GST refunds.

Preparation and filing of refund applications.

Follow-up with authorities to expedite refund processing.

GST Training and Consultation

Conducting workshops or training sessions on GST compliance.

Customized GST consultation for businesses based on their requirements.
